# Physics in Daily Life is a project made to try to bring people closer to science, mostly physics and astronomy, and also teach the history of those things. For example, Hypatia's history, a brilliant woman who got killed just for the crime of thinking differently. And also make people more familiar with the equations used in astrophysics.

The main objective of this website is to show people that physics isn't that monster that they think it is.

In the second page, where there is a search bar for planets in our Solar System, dwarf planets, etc. The constellation that guides to the Hall of Great Minds is the Perseus constellation, and I on purpose made the Algol star brighter. I thought of this because its main representation is the Medusa star, another woman, like Hypatia, who was condemned by society.

The Hall of Great Minds is to show all those amazing people and their contributions. If they created an equation, it was going to be there too.

I have plans for the next update to put galaxies and stars to be explored there too, and also, of course, put a more detailed page for each person in the Hall of Great Minds.
